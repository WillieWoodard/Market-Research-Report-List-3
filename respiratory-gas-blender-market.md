<p><p>The respiratory gas blender market includes two types: manual and electronic. The manual gas blender requires manual adjustment of flow rates and specific gas concentrations, allowing for greater control by the user. On the other hand, electronic gas blenders are automated and provide precise blending of gases based on pre-set parameters, reducing the need for manual intervention. While manual blenders offer flexibility, electronic blenders offer accuracy and convenience. Both types cater to the needs of various medical settings, such as hospitals and clinics, to deliver specific respiratory gas mixtures to patients.</p></p>
