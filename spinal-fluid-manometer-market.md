<p><p>The spinal fluid manometer market consists of two types - All-in-One and Separated Type. All-in-One manometers are designed as a single unit, combining all the necessary components in one device for convenience and ease of use. On the other hand, Separated Type manometers are composed of separate components, where the measuring device is connected to the external pressure source through a tube.
