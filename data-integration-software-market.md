<p><p>Data integration software is available in two market types: on-premise and hosted. On-premise software is installed and operated on local servers, allowing organizations to have full control over their data integration processes. This type requires upfront investments in hardware and maintenance costs. On the other hand, hosted software is cloud-based, allowing users to access and manage their data integration tasks remotely. Hosted software eliminates the need for infrastructure investments and provides scalability and flexibility for users.</p></p>
