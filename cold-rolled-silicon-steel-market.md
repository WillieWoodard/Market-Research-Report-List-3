<p><p>Cold rolled silicon steel, also known as electrical steel, is a type of specialty steel that is commonly used in electrical components and transformers due to its high magnetic permeability and low core loss. It is produced by cold rolling hot rolled steel strips to achieve the desired thickness and magnetic properties. This process helps in reducing the iron loss and improves the efficiency of electrical equipment.</p><p>The global cold rolled silicon steel market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.1% during the forecast period. The growth of the market is driven by the increasing demand for energy-efficient electrical equipment in various industries such as automotive, electronics, and power distribution. Additionally, the rising investments in infrastructure development and renewable energy projects are also contributing to the growth of the market.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the cold rolled silicon steel market include the development of advanced grades of silicon steel with improved magnetic properties and the increasing adoption of grain-oriented silicon steel for high-efficiency transformers.
