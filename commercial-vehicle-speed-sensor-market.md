<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Speed Sensor Market consists of two main types: Hall Type and Magnetic Electric Type. Hall Type sensors use a Hall Effect sensor to measure the speed of the vehicle by detecting the presence of a magnetic field. Magnetic Electric Type sensors generate an electric signal when a magnetic field is present, allowing for accurate speed measurement. Both types are crucial for monitoring and controlling vehicle speed, ensuring the safety and efficiency of commercial vehicles on the road.</p></p>
