<p><p>Post insulators are used in electrical systems to support and isolate power lines from the ground. There are two main types of post insulators: porcelain post insulators and composite material post insulators. Porcelain post insulators are made from traditional materials such as clay and are known for their high mechanical strength and durability. On the other hand, composite material post insulators are made from non-ceramic materials such as fiberglass and silicon rubber, offering advantages such as lightweight and high resistance to pollution.</p></p>
