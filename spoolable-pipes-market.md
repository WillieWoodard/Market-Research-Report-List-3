<p><p>Spoolable pipes are divided into three main market types: Fiber Reinforced, Steel Reinforced, and Hybrid. Fiber reinforced pipes are composed of a high-strength composite material, providing flexibility and corrosion resistance. Steel reinforced pipes use a steel wire to enhance strength and pressure rating. Hybrid pipes combine the benefits of both fiber and steel reinforcements, offering a balance between strength and flexibility. Each market type caters to different industry needs, ranging from oil and gas to water and wastewater applications.</p></p>
