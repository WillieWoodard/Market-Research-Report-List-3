<p><p>Blockchain is a revolutionary technology that is transforming various industries, including infrastructure. In the infrastructure market, there are three types of blockchain: public, private, and others. Public blockchains are decentralized networks where anyone can participate and have equal rights, making them highly transparent and secure. Private blockchains, on the other hand, are restricted to a specific group, providing more control and privacy but sacrificing some decentralization. Lastly, "others" refer to hybrid blockchains or consortium blockchains that combine elements of both public and private blockchains to suit specific needs.</p></p>
