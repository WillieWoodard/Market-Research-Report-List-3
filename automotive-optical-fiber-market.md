<p><strong>The Automotive Optical Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Multimode Fiber</li><li>Single-Mode Fiber</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive optical fiber market is divided into two main types: multimode fiber and single-mode fiber. Multimode fiber is ideal for short distances and high data throughput, making it suitable for applications like in-vehicle networks and infotainment systems. Single-mode fiber, on the other hand, is designed for longer distances and higher bandwidth, making it preferred for applications like autonomous driving and advanced driver-assistance systems. Both types of fiber play crucial roles in enhancing connectivity and communication within automotive systems.</p></p>
