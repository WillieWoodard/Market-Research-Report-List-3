<p><p>PBT yarn market consists of three main types: Partially Oriented Yarn (POY), Fully Drawn Yarn (FDY), and other markets. POY is produced by spinning and drawing melted PBT resin into filaments, which are then wound onto spools. FDY is produced by stretching and drawing POY to align the molecules and improve strength and elasticity. Other markets may include different variations of PBT yarn such as textured yarn, air-jet textured yarn, and bulked continuous filament yarn. These different types cater to the diverse needs of industries such as apparel, home textiles, and automotive.</p></p>
