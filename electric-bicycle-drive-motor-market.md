<p><p>Hub motors are located on the wheel hub of the electric bicycle. They provide direct power to the wheel, making them simple and easy to install. However, they can be heavier and affect the balance of the bike. Mid motors are located near the pedals, providing a more natural riding experience by leveraging the bike's existing gears. They offer better weight distribution and efficiency, but can be more complex to install. Both types have their advantages and are popular in the electric bicycle market.</p></p>
