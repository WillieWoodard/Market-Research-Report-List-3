<p><p>Generic Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients (APIs) are the key chemical components used in the formulation of pharmaceutical drugs. These APIs have the same active ingredients as their branded counterparts but are typically sold under their generic names, due to an expired patent or after the exclusivity period granted to the original drug manufacturer has ended. Generic APIs offer a cost-effective alternative to branded drugs and are widely used in the production of generic medicines.</p><p>The global Generic Active Pharmaceutical Ingredients Market is expected to witness significant growth in the forecast period. Factors contributing to market growth include the increasing demand for cost-effective drugs, the expiration of patents of several branded drugs, rising healthcare expenditures, and the growing prevalence of chronic diseases.</p><p>Additionally, the market is witnessing several trends that are likely to shape its growth trajectory. One such trend is the increasing preference for outsourcing the manufacturing of generics by pharmaceutical companies. This is driven by the need for cost optimization and focusing on core competencies.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing a shift towards the development of complex generics.
