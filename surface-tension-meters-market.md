<p><p>Surface tension meters are devices used to measure the surface tension of liquids. There are two main types of surface tension meters: static surface tension meters and dynamic surface tension meters. </p><p>Static surface tension meters measure the force required to pull a plate or ring from the surface of a liquid at rest, while dynamic surface tension meters measure the force required to keep a probe moving through the surface of a liquid in motion. Both types of meters are important tools for various industries such as pharmaceuticals, chemicals, and food and beverage.</p></p>
