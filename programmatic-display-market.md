<p><p>The programmatic display market comprises three types: Real Time Bidding (RTB), Programmatic Direct, and Private Exchange Buying (PMP). RTB automates the buying and selling of individual ad impressions in real-time auctions. Programmatic Direct enables purchasing ad inventory directly from publishers. PMP refers to invitation-only ad marketplaces where advertisers and publishers negotiate and sell ad inventory programmatically. In these marketplaces, ad inventory is private and selective, offering higher quality and targeted audiences. Programmatic technologies simplify and streamline the buying and selling of ad space, making the process more efficient and effective for advertisers and publishers.</p></p>
