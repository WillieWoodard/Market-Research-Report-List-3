<p><p>Biopharmaceutical cold chain logistics market can be divided into three main types: active cold chain, passive cold chain, and others. Active cold chain involves the use of powered cooling units to maintain a specific temperature in transit, while passive cold chain relies on insulation and refrigerants to keep products within the required temperature range. The "others" category includes alternative methods such as temperature-controlled packaging and temperature monitoring devices to ensure the safe transportation of biopharmaceutical products.</p></p>
