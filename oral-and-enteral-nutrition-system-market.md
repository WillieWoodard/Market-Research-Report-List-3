<p><p>Oral and enteral nutrition system refers to the method of providing nutrition through the gastrointestinal tract. <p><strong>The Oral and Enteral Nutrition System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Enteral Feeding Pumps</li><li>Enteral Feeding Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Oral and Enteral Nutrition System market comprises two main types: Enteral Feeding Pumps and Enteral Feeding Tubes. Enteral Feeding Pumps are medical devices used to deliver liquid nutrition directly into the digestive tract, typically through a tube. They ensure a regulated and controlled feeding process. On the other hand, Enteral Feeding Tubes are flexible tubes inserted through the nose, mouth, or abdomen into the stomach or intestines to provide nutrition when individuals are unable to consume food orally. These products are essential in facilitating adequate nutrition for patients with difficulties in consuming food by mouth.</p></p>
