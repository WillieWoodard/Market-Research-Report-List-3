<p><p>Opto-electronics chip testers come in two main types: Semi Automated and Full Automated. In Semi Automated testers, some tasks are still performed manually by operators, such as loading and unloading chips, setting up test parameters, and interpreting results. On the other hand, Full Automated testers are capable of performing all testing processes without human intervention, from chip insertion to data analysis. Full Automated testers are typically more efficient and accurate but may require higher initial investment costs compared to Semi Automated testers.</p></p>
