<p><p>Automatic infrared motion sensor trash cans come in three main market types: knee induction, hand induction, and foot induction. Knee induction trash cans are operated by simply moving your knee towards the sensor to open the lid. Hand induction trash cans open when a hand is waved in front of the sensor. Foot induction trash cans open when the foot is placed near the sensor. These different types cater to different preferences and needs, providing convenient and hygienic waste disposal options.</p></p>
