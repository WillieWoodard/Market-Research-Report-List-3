<p><ul><li>Mechanical Suspension Seats</li><li>Air Suspension Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Farm tractor seats can be categorized into two main types: Mechanical Suspension Seats and Air Suspension Seats. Mechanical Suspension Seats are equipped with a spring or hydraulic system that absorbs shocks and vibrations, providing a comfortable ride for the operator. On the other hand, Air Suspension Seats use compressed air to adjust the seat height and provide superior isolation from bumps and jolts. Both types offer ergonomic support and reduce fatigue for operators during long hours of use in agricultural settings.</p></p>
