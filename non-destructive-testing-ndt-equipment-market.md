<p><p>Non Destructive Testing (NDT) Equipment Market includes various types of testing methods such as Eddy Current Testing, Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing, Ultrasonic Testing and Other methods. Eddy Current Testing uses electromagnetic induction to detect surface and near-surface flaws in conductive materials. Magnetic Flux Leakage Testing relies on magnetic fields to detect discontinuities in ferromagnetic materials. Ultrasonic Testing utilizes high frequency sound waves to inspect internal and surface defects in materials. Other methods include radiographic testing, visual inspection, and dye penetrant testing. These NDT methods help ensure the quality and reliability of materials without causing damage to the tested objects.</p></p>
