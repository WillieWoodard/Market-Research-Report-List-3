<p><ul><li>Band Pass Filter</li><li>Band Stop Filter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The WIFI and LTE Coexistence Filter market includes two main types of filters: Band Pass Filters and Band Stop Filters. Band Pass Filters allow a certain range of frequencies to pass through, while blocking all others, ideal for isolating specific frequency bands in WIFI and LTE signals. On the other hand, Band Stop Filters block a specific range of frequencies, allowing all others to pass through, effectively reducing interference between WIFI and LTE signals. Both types of filters play a critical role in optimizing signal quality and minimizing interference in wireless communication systems.</p></p>
