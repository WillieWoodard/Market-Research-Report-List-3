Additionally, the market is witnessing increased collaboration between healthcare providers and insurance companies to offer medical second opinion services as part of health insurance packages, further fueling market growth.</p><p>Overall, the medical second opinion services market is poised for substantial growth in the coming years, driven by increasing patient awareness, technological advancements, and supportive government initiatives.</p></p>
