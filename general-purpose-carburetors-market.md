<p><ul><li>Float-Feed Carburetor</li><li>Diaphragm Carburetor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>General purpose carburetors are commonly categorized into two types: Float-Feed Carburetor and Diaphragm Carburetor. Float-Feed Carburetor works by using a float mechanism to control the fuel flow into the engine, ensuring a consistent fuel-air mixture. On the other hand, Diaphragm Carburetor uses a flexible diaphragm to regulate fuel flow, offering better fuel efficiency and performance. Both types are popular choices for small engines in applications such as lawnmowers, chainsaws, and motorcycles.</p></p>
