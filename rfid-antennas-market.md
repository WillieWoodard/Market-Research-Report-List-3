<p><strong>The RFID Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active RFID Antennas</li><li>Passive RFID Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>RFID antennas market consists of two main types, active and passive. Active RFID antennas require a power source to transmit signals and are capable of longer read ranges, making them ideal for tracking high-value items. Passive RFID antennas, on the other hand, rely on the energy emitted by RFID readers to send signals, making them more cost-effective and suitable for tracking everyday items. Both types have their own advantages and applications, catering to different needs in the growing RFID technology market.</p></p>
