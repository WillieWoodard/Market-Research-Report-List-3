<p><p>Automotive force sensors can be categorized into different types based on their working principles. Capacitive force sensors measure force by detecting changes in capacitance, while Piezo-resistive force sensors utilize the piezoelectric effect to convert mechanical stress into electrical signals. Other types of force sensors may include optical, strain gauge, and magnetic sensors. Each of these sensor types offers unique advantages and can be used in various automotive applications to measure force, pressure, or stress accurately.</p></p>
