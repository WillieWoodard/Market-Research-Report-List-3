<p><strong>The Airplane Bearing Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roller</li><li>Ball</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The airplane bearing market includes two main types: roller bearings and ball bearings. Roller bearings use cylindrical rolling elements to reduce friction and support radial and axial loads, making them suitable for high-speed applications. Ball bearings, on the other hand, use spherical rolling elements to support radial and axial loads, providing smooth and precise movement in various aircraft components. Both roller and ball bearings play a crucial role in ensuring the efficient and reliable operation of airplane systems.</p></p>
