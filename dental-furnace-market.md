<p><ul><li>Sintering Furnaces</li><li>Burnout Furnaces</li><li>Production Furnaces</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Dental furnaces are categorized into three main types: sintering furnaces, burnout furnaces, and production furnaces. Sintering furnaces are used to fuse dental prosthetics together, while burnout furnaces are utilized for eliminating wax residue from molds before casting. Production furnaces are designed for the mass production of dental products. Each type of furnace serves a specific purpose in the dental industry, contributing to the efficient and accurate fabrication of dental restorations and prosthetics.</p></p>
