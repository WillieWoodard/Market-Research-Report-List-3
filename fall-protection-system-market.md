Harnesses are worn by workers and are connected to an anchor point to prevent them from falling. Lanyards are used to connect the harness to the anchorage point, while self-retracting lifelines enable workers to move around more freely while still staying securely attached. Belts are similar to harnesses but are smaller and lighter. Other types of fall protection systems may include guardrails, nets, and anchors.</p></p>
