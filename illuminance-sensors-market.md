<p><ul><li>Surface Mount Type</li><li>SIP Type</li><li>COB (Chip On Board) Type</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface mount type illuminance sensors are mounted directly on the surface of a PCB, making them compact and suitable for space-constrained applications. SIP type sensors are designed for through-hole mounting, providing stability and durability. COB (Chip On Board) type sensors integrate the sensor directly onto the PCB, offering high levels of integration and functionality. Others market include sensors with unique mounting options or features that do not fit into the standard categories mentioned above. These different types cater to a variety of applications and requirements in the illuminance sensor market.</p></p>
