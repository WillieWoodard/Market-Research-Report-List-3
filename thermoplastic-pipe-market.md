The rise in infrastructure development projects, such as highways, airports, and residential buildings, further contributes to the market expansion.</p><p>Moreover, the increasing use of thermoplastic pipes in industries such as chemical, mining, and agriculture for fluid transmission and irrigation systems is augmenting market growth.
