<p><p>PET scan devices market consists of two types of devices: PET scanners and PET-CT scanners. PET scanners are specifically designed for positron emission tomography, a medical imaging technique that detects gamma rays emitted by a radioactive tracer in the body. These scanners are used to visualize and evaluate biological processes, such as metabolism and blood flow, aiding in the diagnosis and monitoring of various diseases. On the other hand, PET-CT scanners combine PET and computed tomography technologies, providing both structural and functional imaging capabilities for improved accuracy in detecting cancers and assessing treatment response.</p></p>
