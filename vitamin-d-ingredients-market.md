<p><p>Vitamin D ingredients market is divided into two primary types - Vitamin D3 and Vitamin D2. Vitamin D3, also known as cholecalciferol, is derived from animal sources such as fish oils and egg yolks. It is considered more effective in raising blood levels of vitamin D in humans. On the other hand, Vitamin D2, also known as ergocalciferol, is obtained from plant sources like mushrooms. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining strong bones, teeth, and overall good health.</p></p>
