<p><p>The dry bulk material handling system market comprises two main types: loading equipment and conveying equipment. Loading equipment refers to machinery used to load bulk materials onto trucks, ships, or trains. It includes equipment such as loaders, stackers, and reclaimers. On the other hand, conveying equipment involves the transportation of bulk materials within a facility using various methods, including conveyor belts, pneumatic systems, and screw conveyors. Both loading and conveying equipment play vital roles in ensuring efficient and safe handling of dry bulk materials in industries like mining, construction, and agriculture.</p></p>
