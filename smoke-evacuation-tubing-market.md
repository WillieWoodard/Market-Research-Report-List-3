<p><p>Smoke evacuation tubing is a medical device used to remove smoke, aerosols, and odors generated during surgical procedures. It is connected to surgical instruments to capture and evacuate the smoke produced by electrocautery, laser, or ultrasonic devices, thereby preventing exposure to harmful plume particles. The tubing is usually flexible and comes in various lengths and diameters to accommodate different surgical setups.</p><p>The smoke evacuation tubing market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing number of surgical procedures, technological advancements in surgical instruments, and growing awareness about the hazards of surgical smoke are the key factors driving market growth. One such trend is the integration of smoke evacuation systems with surgical robots.
