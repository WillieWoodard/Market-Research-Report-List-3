Radiography systems use X-rays to create images of the internal structures of animals. Ultrasound imaging systems use sound waves to generate images of organs and tissues. Computed tomography imaging systems produce detailed cross-sectional images. Video endoscopy imaging systems allow for visual examination of internal body parts. Magnetic resonance imaging systems use strong magnetic fields and radio waves to produce detailed images of the body.</p></p>
