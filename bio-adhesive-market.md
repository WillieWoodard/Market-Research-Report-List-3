<p><p>Bio adhesives can be categorized into two main types: Plant-based adhesives and Animal-based adhesives. Plant-based adhesives are derived from natural sources such as soybeans, corn, and starch, making them eco-friendly and sustainable. On the other hand, Animal-based adhesives are derived from proteins like collagen and gelatin obtained from animal tissues. Both types of adhesives are gaining popularity in various industries due to their biodegradability, non-toxic nature, and potential to reduce environmental impact compared to traditional synthetic adhesives.</p></p>
