<p><p>The Automotive Heat Pump System Market includes various types such as outside heat exchanger, inside condenser, evaporator, compressor, and others. The outside heat exchanger is responsible for transferring heat from the environment to the refrigerant. The inside condenser releases heat inside the vehicle. The evaporator absorbs heat from the inside of the vehicle. The compressor increases the pressure and temperature of the refrigerant. Other components in the market may include expansion valves, refrigerant lines, and sensors for efficient heat transfer in automotive systems.</p></p>
