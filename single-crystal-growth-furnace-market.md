<p><ul><li>Czochralski Process (Cz) Furnace</li><li>Float Zone (FZ) Furnace</li><li>Bridgman Furnace</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single Crystal Growth Furnace market includes various types, such as Czochralski Process (Cz) Furnace, which is the most widely used method for growing single crystals with high purity and quality. Float Zone (FZ) Furnace is another method that involves melting a small region of the sample and pulling it through a controlled atmosphere, resulting in extremely pure crystals. Bridgman Furnace is used for growing crystals by controlling the temperature gradient and directional solidification. Other methods may include Gradient Freeze and Vertical Bridgman Furnaces.</p></p>
