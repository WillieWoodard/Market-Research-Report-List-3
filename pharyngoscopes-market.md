<p><p>Pharyngoscopes are medical devices used to examine the pharynx and throat. There are two main types: straight pharyngoscopes and bent pharyngoscopes. Straight pharyngoscopes are designed with a straight shaft, allowing for a direct view of the pharynx. Both types offer healthcare professionals different options for conducting thorough examinations and selecting the most suitable tool based on the patient's needs and anatomical factors.</p></p>
