<p><p>Trivalent chromium plating refers to the process of electroplating a layer of trivalent chromium onto a metal surface. It is an eco-friendly alternative to hexavalent chromium plating, which is known to be toxic and carcinogenic. Trivalent chromium plating offers several advantages, such as enhanced corrosion resistance, improved hardness, and increased wear resistance. It is widely used in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, electrical and electronics, and others.</p><p>The trivalent chromium plating market is expected to witness significant growth during the forecast period. The increasing awareness about the harmful effects of hexavalent chromium plating and the implementation of stringent environmental regulations are major factors driving the adoption of trivalent chromium plating.
