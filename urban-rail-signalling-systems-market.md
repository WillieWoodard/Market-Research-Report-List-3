<p><ul><li>Semi-automatic Train Operation</li><li>Driverless Train Operation</li><li>Unattended Train Operation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Urban Rail Signalling Systems have three main market types: Semi-automatic Train Operation, Driverless Train Operation, and Unattended Train Operation. Semi-automatic Train Operation involves limited human intervention with the train's operation. Driverless Train Operation eliminates the need for a human driver, with the system controlling the train's movement. Unattended Train Operation takes it a step further, operating completely autonomously without any onboard staff. These systems offer increased efficiency, safety, and reliability in urban rail transportation.</p></p>
