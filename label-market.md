<p><p>Label Market Types refer to different adhesive methods for labels on products. Pressure Sensitive labels have a self-adhesive backing, making them easy to apply. Glue-applied labels require additional adhesive for application. Heat-Shrink labels shrink to fit the product when heat is applied. Stretch Sleeve labels stretch and conform to the product's shape. In-mold Labels are inserted into the mold before the product is formed, creating a seamless label. Each type offers unique benefits and applications for various industries.</p></p>
