<p><p>Positron Emission Tomography (PET) Systems market types include Direct Imaging, Indirect Imaging, and Alternative Imaging. Direct Imaging involves the direct detection of gamma rays emitted by positron-emitting radionuclides. Indirect Imaging uses scintillation detectors to convert gamma ray signals into electrical signals for image reconstruction. Alternative Imaging methods may involve new technologies or approaches to improve image quality or reduce exposure to radiation. Each type of imaging has its advantages and limitations in the field of PET systems.</p></p>
