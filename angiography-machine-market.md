<p><p>The Angiography Machine market consists of various types of machines used for diagnostic imaging of blood vessels. X-ray Angiography employs X-ray technology to produce detailed images of blood vessels. CT Angiography utilizes computed tomography scanning to create 3D images of blood vessels. Magnetic Resonance Angiography employs magnetic fields and radio waves to capture detailed images of blood vessels. MR-CT Angiography combines magnetic resonance and computed tomography imaging techniques to produce highly precise vascular images. These different types provide physicians with options for specific patient needs and enable accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
