<p><ul><li>Horizontal Installation</li><li>Vertical Installation</li><li>Recessed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nonre-circulating air curtains are divided into three market types based on their installation method: horizontal, vertical, and recessed. Horizontal installations are typically seen above entrances where the air flow is directed straight down. Vertical installations are placed on the sides of entrances to create a barrier against outside air. Recessed installations are integrated into the ceiling for a more discreet appearance. Each type is designed to effectively prevent the exchange of indoor and outdoor air while also providing energy savings and maintaining a comfortable indoor environment.</p></p>
