<p><strong>The Engine Oils Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-grade</li><li>Multi-grade</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Engine oils are classified into two main types - single-grade and multi-grade. Single-grade oils have a uniform viscosity rating, such as SAE 30 or SAE 40, meaning they maintain consistent flow characteristics in a specific temperature range. Multi-grade oils, like SAE 10W-40, have a range of viscosity that allows them to perform well in both cold and hot temperatures. Single-grade oils are often used in older vehicles, while multi-grade oils are more common in modern engines for year-round protection.</p></p>
