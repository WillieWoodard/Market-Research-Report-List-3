<p><p>Explosion proof pressure transducers come in various types to suit different applications. Relative pressure type measures pressure relative to atmospheric pressure, while absolute pressure type measures pressure relative to a full vacuum. Differential pressure type measures the difference in pressure between two points. Other market types may include compound pressure transducers, which can measure both positive and negative pressures, and vacuum pressure transducers, which specifically measure low-pressure levels. Each type is designed to provide accurate pressure readings in hazardous environments where explosions are a concern.</p></p>
