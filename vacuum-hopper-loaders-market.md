<p><p>Vacuum hopper loaders are commonly categorized into two types: Integral Type and Split Type. Integral Type loaders are designed with the vacuum hopper and material receiver combined into a single unit, offering a compact and space-saving solution. On the other hand, Split Type loaders have a separate vacuum hopper and material receiver, providing more flexibility in installation and maintenance. Each type has its own unique advantages, catering to different preferences and requirements in the market.</p></p>
