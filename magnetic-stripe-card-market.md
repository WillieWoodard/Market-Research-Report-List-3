<p><strong>The Magnetic Stripe Card Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High Coercivity</li><li>Low Coercivity</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Magnetic stripe cards come in two main types, high coercivity, and low coercivity. High coercivity cards have a stronger magnetic field and are more durable, making them ideal for long-term or high-security applications such as access control or financial transactions. Low coercivity cards have a weaker magnetic field and are typically used for short-term applications like hotel room keys or gift cards. The choice between high or low coercivity cards depends on the level of security and durability required for the specific use case.</p></p>
