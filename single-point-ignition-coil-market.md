<p><p>Single Point Ignition Coil market includes three types: Ordinary Ignition Coil, Plug Top Ignition Coil, and Pen Ignition Coil. Ordinary Ignition Coils are traditional cylindrical coils used in older vehicles. Plug Top Ignition Coils are shaped like a spark plug and are directly attached to the spark plug itself. Pen Ignition Coils are compact coils that are mounted on the engine block. Each type caters to different vehicle designs and performance needs, offering a range of options for ignition systems in the automotive market.</p></p>
