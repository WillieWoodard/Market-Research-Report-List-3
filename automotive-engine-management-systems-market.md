<p><strong>The Automotive Engine Management Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>MPFi Systems</li><li>GDi Systems</li><li>CRDi System</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Engine Management Systems include MPFi Systems (Multi-Point Fuel Injection), GDi Systems (Gasoline Direct Injection), CRDi Systems (Common Rail Diesel Injection), and other systems. MPFi Systems use multiple fuel injectors to deliver fuel to each cylinder for improved efficiency. GDi Systems directly inject fuel into the combustion chamber for better performance. CRDi Systems use a common fuel rail to supply fuel at high pressure, enhancing fuel efficiency. Other systems may include electronic throttle control, variable valve timing, and turbocharging for optimal engine performance.</p></p>
