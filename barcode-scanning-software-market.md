<p><p>Barcode scanning software market can be classified into two types: on-premise and cloud-based. On-premise software is installed and operates locally on the user's own hardware system. It provides full control and customization but requires maintenance and regular updates. On the other hand, cloud-based software is hosted on remote servers and accessed through the internet. It offers scalability, easier updates, and reduces hardware costs. Users can access data from anywhere and the software is maintained by the provider, offering convenience and flexibility.</p></p>
