<p><p>Non-ferrous metal castings market can be categorized into two main types: light metal and heavy metal. Light metal castings include aluminum, magnesium, and titanium, which are known for their low density, good corrosion resistance, and high strength-to-weight ratio. On the other hand, heavy metal castings like copper, zinc, and lead are denser, more malleable, and have better thermal and electrical conductivity. Both light and heavy metal castings play crucial roles in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and construction.</p></p>
