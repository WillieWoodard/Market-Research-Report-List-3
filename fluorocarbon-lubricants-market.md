<p><p>Fluorocarbon lubricants are categorized into three main types: oils, waxes, and greases. Fluorocarbon oils are liquid lubricants that provide a thin film of protection, ideal for low to medium load applications. Fluorocarbon waxes are solid lubricants that offer excellent wear protection for heavy-duty applications. Fluorocarbon greases are semisolid lubricants that contain a thickening agent to provide added lubrication for high load and extreme temperature conditions. Each type of lubricant offers different benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the application.</p></p>
