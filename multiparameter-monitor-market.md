<p><strong>The Multiparameter Monitor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>High-acuity Monitors</li><li>Mid-acuity Monitors</li><li>Low-acuity Monitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Multiparameter monitors can be categorized into three main types based on acuity level: high-acuity monitors, mid-acuity monitors, and low-acuity monitors. High-acuity monitors are designed for use in intensive care units and other critical care settings, providing comprehensive monitoring capabilities for patients with serious medical conditions. Mid-acuity monitors are typically used in step-down units and other intermediate care settings, offering a more limited range of monitoring functions. Low-acuity monitors are used in general wards and outpatient settings, providing basic vital sign monitoring for stable patients.</p></p>
