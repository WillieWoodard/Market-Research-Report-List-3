<p><p>The Dual Access Catheter market includes two types: the Two Distal Radiopaque Markers Spaced and the Single Radiopaque Marker At Distal Tip. In the Two Distal Radiopaque Markers Spaced type, there are two markers placed at a certain distance from each other. These markers are visible under X-ray, helping medical professionals accurately position the catheter. In the Single Radiopaque Marker At Distal Tip type, there is only one marker present at the distal tip of the catheter. This marker assists in identifying the exact location of the catheter during procedures.</p></p>
