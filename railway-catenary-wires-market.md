<p><p>Railway catenary wires are classified into three main types based on their structure and design: Simple Catenary, Stiched Catenary, and Compound Catenary. Simple Catenary consists of a single wire suspended from multiple supports. Stitched Catenary includes multiple wires stitched together to form a strong structure. Compound Catenary combines both simple and stitched elements to provide enhanced stability and support for heavier loads. Each type caters to different requirements and specifications in the railway industry, offering unique benefits and capabilities for overhead electrification systems.</p></p>
