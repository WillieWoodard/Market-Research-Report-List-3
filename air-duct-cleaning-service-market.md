<p><p>The air duct cleaning service market can be categorized into two main types: Whole-Home and Split Systems. Whole-Home air duct cleaning refers to the comprehensive cleaning of all air ducts, including those connected to heating, ventilation, and air conditioning (HVAC) systems throughout the entire house. On the other hand, Split Systems air duct cleaning focuses on the cleaning of air ducts in specific areas or rooms served by individual HVAC units, typically found in apartment buildings, offices, or commercial spaces. These services aim to improve indoor air quality by removing dust, debris, and contaminants from the ductwork.</p></p>
