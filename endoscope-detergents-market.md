<p><p>Endoscope detergents are classified into three main types: acid detergent, neutral detergent, and alkaline detergent. Acid detergents are used for removing inorganic deposits such as scale and rust. Neutral detergents are effective for general cleaning and removing organic debris. Alkaline detergents are used to dissolve fats, oils, and proteins. Each type of detergent is formulated to target specific types of contaminants found on endoscopes, ensuring thorough cleaning and disinfection of these delicate instruments.</p></p>
