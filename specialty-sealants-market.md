<p><p>Specialty sealants market includes various types such as Silicone sealants, Silyl-Modified sealants, Polyurethane sealants, and Polysulfides sealants. Silicone sealants are the most commonly used type due to their resistance to high temperatures and UV exposure. Silyl-Modified sealants offer excellent adhesion properties and low VOC emissions. Polyurethane sealants provide flexibility and durability. Polysulfides sealants are known for their chemical resistance and waterproofing properties. Each type caters to specific needs in industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
