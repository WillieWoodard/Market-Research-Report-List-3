<p><p>Aramid yarns are categorized into two main types: Meta-Aramid Yarn and Para-Aramid Yarn. Meta-aramid yarn is known for its heat resistance and flame retardant properties, making it suitable for applications in protective clothing and industrial equipment. On the other hand, Para-Aramid yarn is highly durable and has high tensile strength, making it ideal for use in bulletproof vests, ropes, and tires. Both types of aramid yarns are utilized in various industries for their unique properties and performance characteristics.</p></p>
