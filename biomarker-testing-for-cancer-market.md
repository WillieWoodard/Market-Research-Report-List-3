<p><p>Biomarker testing plays a crucial role in the diagnosis, management, and treatment of cancer. The market for biomarker testing can be categorized into three main types: protein biomarkers, genetic biomarkers, and others. Protein biomarkers are molecules found in the blood or tissue that indicate the presence or progression of cancer. Genetic biomarkers, on the other hand, are specific DNA mutations or alterations that can predict a person's risk of developing cancer. Other biomarkers encompass a range of indicators, such as circulating tumor cells or imaging-based biomarkers, which provide additional information regarding cancer diagnosis or treatment response.</p></p>
