<p><p>Solvent recovery and recycling refer to the process of extracting and reusing solvents in various industries, reducing waste and promoting sustainability. On-site solvent recycling involves the implementation of recycling systems within the production facilities itself, allowing immediate solvent reclamation and reuse. Off-site solvent recycling, on the other hand, requires the transportation of used solvents to external specialized recycling facilities. Both types contribute to reducing environmental impact, conserving resources, and minimizing disposal costs, but differ in terms of location and level of control over the recycling process.</p></p>
