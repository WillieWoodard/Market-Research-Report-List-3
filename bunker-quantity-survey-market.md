<p><p>Bunker Quantity Survey refers to the process of determining and calculating the quantity of fuel oil or bunker oil supplied to a ship or vessel. The survey is typically conducted by independent third-party surveyors who use specialized equipment and methodologies to provide an accurate measurement.</p><p>The Bunker Quantity Survey Market has been experiencing significant growth in recent years.
