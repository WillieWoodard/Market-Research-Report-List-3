<p><p>Styrene Isoprene Styrene (SIS) is a type of thermoplastic elastomer used in various industries. It comes in different market types: linear and radial. Linear SIS is a linear chain of SIS polymers and is commonly used in adhesive applications due to its excellent tack and cohesive properties. On the other hand, radial SIS has a three-dimensional structure, which provides better mechanical strength and is often applied in products like rubber modified asphalt and compound blends. These market types offer different properties and applications based on their structure.</p></p>
