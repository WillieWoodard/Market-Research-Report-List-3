<p><ul><li>Locking Casters</li><li>Kingpin-less Casters</li><li>Hollow Kingpin Casters</li><li>Plate Casters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Locking casters have a mechanism that prevents the wheels from rolling, providing stability when the equipment is in use. Kingpin-less casters have a single kingpin, allowing for easier steering and maintenance. Hollow kingpin casters have a hollow center pin, making them lighter and more durable. Plate casters are versatile and can be bolted or welded onto equipment. Each type of caster serves a specific purpose and is designed to meet the unique needs of healthcare facilities and medical equipment.</p></p>
