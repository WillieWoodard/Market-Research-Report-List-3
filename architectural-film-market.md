Glass Fabric offers transparency and light diffusion, making it ideal for building facades. Polyester Fabric (PES) is known for its durability and flexibility, making it suitable for tensioned membrane structures. ETFE Sheeting combines lightweight and high light transmission, making it popular for large-scale architectural projects. Other materials in the market include PVC and PTFE, each offering unique properties to meet different architectural design needs.</p></p>
