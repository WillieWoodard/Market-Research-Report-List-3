<p><p>Sodium silicate glass is categorized into three main types based on the molecular weight of the silicate. Medium molecular weight (Medium Mol) sodium silicate is commonly used in adhesives and detergents. High molecular weight (High Mol) sodium silicate is preferred for applications requiring higher viscosity and heat resistance, such as in the automotive and construction industries. Extra-high molecular weight (Extra-high Mol) sodium silicate is used in industries requiring even greater strength and durability, such as in the manufacturing of ceramics and refractories.</p></p>
