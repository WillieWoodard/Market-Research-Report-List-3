<p><p>SMD ECG monitors are available in two main market types: Holter ECG testers and static ECG testers. Holter ECG testers are portable devices that are worn by a patient for an extended period of time to continuously monitor their heart activity. Static ECG testers, on the other hand, are stationary devices that are used for one-time ECG readings. Both types of ECG monitors play a crucial role in diagnosing and monitoring heart conditions, providing healthcare professionals with valuable data for treatment planning.</p></p>
