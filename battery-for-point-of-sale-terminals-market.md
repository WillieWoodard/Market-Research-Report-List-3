<p><p>Lead-Acid Batteries are commonly used in point of sale terminals due to their cost-effectiveness and reliability. However, they are bulky and have a shorter lifespan compared to other types. Li-Ion Batteries are preferred for their lightweight design and longer lifespan, making them ideal for portable terminals. Ni-Cd Batteries are known for their high energy density and durability, but they are less commonly used in point of sale terminals due to concerns about cadmium toxicity. Each type of battery offers different advantages and considerations for businesses looking to power their point of sale terminals.</p></p>
