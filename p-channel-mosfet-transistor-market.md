<p><p>P-channel MOSFET transistors are classified into two main types: Enhancement Type and Depletion Type. Enhancement Type transistors require a positive voltage between the gate and source to create a conductive channel, while Depletion Type transistors naturally have a conductive channel without any external voltage applied. The market for Enhancement Type transistors tends to be larger due to their lower power consumption and better performance in most applications, while Depletion Type transistors are typically used in specialized applications requiring specific characteristics.</p></p>
