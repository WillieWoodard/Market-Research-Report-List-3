<p><p>ECG equipment and management system market includes various types of devices such as Holter monitors, resting ECG systems, stress ECG monitors, and event monitoring systems. Holter monitors are portable devices used for continuous monitoring of heart activity. Resting ECG systems record the heart's electrical activity during a period of rest. Stress ECG monitors are used during physical activity to assess heart function. Event monitoring systems are used for short-term monitoring to capture irregular heart rhythms. These devices play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing heart conditions.</p></p>
