<p><p>Rectangular Hollow Section Steel, also known as RHS, is available in two main market types: Welded RHS and Seamless RHS. Welded RHS is manufactured by welding strips of steel together, providing structural strength and durability. Seamless RHS, on the other hand, is produced without any welding, resulting in a smooth and uniform finish. Both market types offer excellent corrosion resistance and are commonly used in construction, infrastructure, and manufacturing industries for various structural applications.</p></p>
