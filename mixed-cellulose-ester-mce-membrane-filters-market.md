<p><p>Mixed Cellulose Ester (MCE) Membrane Filters are commonly available in two types - MCE Gridded Membrane Filters and MCE Plain Membrane Filters. MCE Gridded Membrane Filters have a grid pattern on the surface, which assists in accurate particle counting and location identification. On the other hand, MCE Plain Membrane Filters do not have a grid pattern and are commonly used for general filtration purposes. Both types of MCE Membrane Filters are widely used in various industries for applications such as microbiology, environmental monitoring, and water quality analysis.</p></p>
