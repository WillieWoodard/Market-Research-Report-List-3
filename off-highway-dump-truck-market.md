<p><p>Off-highway dump trucks are categorized into two main types - rigid frame and articulating frame. Rigid frame dump trucks have a solid frame structure, providing more stability and durability for heavy-duty applications. In comparison, articulating frame dump trucks have a pivot point between the cab and the dump box, allowing for increased maneuverability on rough terrains. Both types of dump trucks are commonly used in construction, mining, and other industries where heavy materials need to be transported efficiently and safely.</p></p>
