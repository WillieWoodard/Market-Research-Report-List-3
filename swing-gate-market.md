<p><ul><li>Mechanical</li><li>Semi-Automatic</li><li>Automatic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Swing gate market can be classified into three types based on the level of automation: Mechanical, Semi-Automatic, and Automatic. Mechanical swing gates are operated manually, requiring physical effort to open and close. Semi-automatic swing gates use a combination of manual and automated mechanisms, such as remote controls or keypads. Fully automatic swing gates are powered by electric motors and open and close on their own with the push of a button or sensor activation. Each type caters to different preferences and needs in terms of convenience and security.</p></p>
