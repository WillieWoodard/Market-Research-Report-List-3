<p><p>There are two main types of wireless home security cameras in the market: Wi-fi and IP cameras. IP cameras, on the other hand, use internet protocol technology to transmit data over a network, allowing for remote viewing and monitoring. Both types offer advanced features like motion detection, night vision, and two-way audio, making them popular choices for home security systems.</p></p>
