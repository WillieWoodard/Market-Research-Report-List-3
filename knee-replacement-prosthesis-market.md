<p><p>The Knee Replacement Prosthesis market consists of three main types: Tibial Component, Femoral Component, and Patella Component. The Tibial Component is a type of knee replacement that involves replacing the damaged tibial plateau with a artificial implant. The Femoral Component replaces the damaged end of the femur bone with a prosthetic implant. Lastly, the Patella Component is used to replace the damaged patella, also known as the knee cap. These different components provide solutions for patients with varying degrees of knee damage in order to improve mobility and reduce pain.</p></p>
