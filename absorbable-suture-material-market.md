<p><p>The absorbable suture material market consists of two types: synthetic material and natural material. Synthetic material, made from polymers such as polylactic acid and polyglycolic acid, are widely used due to their high tensile strength and low tissue reactivity. Natural materials, derived from sources like silk or gut, are also used for absorbable sutures. These materials have the advantage of being easily absorbed by the body, reducing the need for suture removal. Both synthetic and natural materials cater to the varying needs of clinicians and patients in surgical procedures.</p></p>
