<p><p>Aluminum mesh is widely available in various types including square mesh, hexagonal mesh, triangular mesh, and twisted mesh. Square mesh is characterized by its evenly spaced squares, providing a uniform appearance and structural support. Hexagonal mesh features honeycomb-shaped openings, offering flexibility and versatility for different applications. Triangular mesh is known for its triangular-shaped openings, ideal for filtering and sieving purposes. Twisted mesh is composed of intertwining wires, resulting in a strong and durable structure suitable for heavy-duty applications.</p></p>
