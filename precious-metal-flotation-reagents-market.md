<p><strong>The Precious Metal Flotation Reagents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Collector</li><li>Activator</li><li>Inhibitor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Precious metal flotation reagents are chemicals used in the process of separating valuable metals from their ores. Collectors are chemicals that enhance the attachment of precious metals to bubbles, facilitating their removal from the ore. Activators are compounds that increase the activity of collectors, leading to more efficient metal recovery. Inhibitors, on the other hand, prevent the attachment of certain minerals to the bubbles, improving the selectivity of the flotation process. These three types of reagents play a crucial role in optimizing precious metal recovery in mining operations.</p></p>
