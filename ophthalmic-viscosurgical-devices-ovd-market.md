<p><strong>The Ophthalmic Viscosurgical Devices (OVD) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cohesive OVD</li><li>Dispersive OVD</li><li>Combined</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ophthalmic Viscosurgical Devices (OVD) are commonly used during eye surgeries to maintain space, protect tissue, and enhance surgical efficiency. There are three main types of OVDs in the market. Cohesive OVDs have a thick, gel-like consistency that adheres well to the area being treated, preventing the loss of space. Dispersive OVDs have a thinner consistency and disperse evenly, protecting tissue from damage caused by surgical instruments. Combined OVDs are a combination of cohesive and dispersive OVDs, providing both adherence and protection properties.</p></p>
