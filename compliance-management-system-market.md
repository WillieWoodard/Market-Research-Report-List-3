<p><p>The Compliance Management System (CMS) market can be categorized into two types: cloud-based and on-premise. Cloud-based CMS refers to a system that is hosted and operated on a remote server, allowing users to access it via the internet. This type of system offers flexibility, easy scalability, and reduced infrastructure costs. On the other hand, an on-premise CMS is installed and managed on local servers within the organization's premises. This type provides better control over data and customization options but may require higher initial investments and maintenance efforts.</p></p>
