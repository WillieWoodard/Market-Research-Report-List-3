<p><p>The automotive GPS antenna market consists of two main types: internal antennas and external antennas. Internal antennas are built into the vehicle and are generally more compact and discreet. They offer the advantage of being less susceptible to damage and theft. On the other hand, external antennas are mounted on the outside of the vehicle and tend to provide stronger and more reliable signal reception. They are commonly used in situations where a stronger GPS signal is necessary, such as in rural or mountainous areas.</p></p>
