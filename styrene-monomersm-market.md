<p><strong>The Styrene Monomer(SM) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ethylbenzene Dehydrogenation Method</li><li>Ethylbenzene Oxidation Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Styrene Monomer (SM) is produced using two main methods: Ethylbenzene Dehydrogenation Method and Ethylbenzene Oxidation Method. The Ethylbenzene Dehydrogenation Method involves converting ethylbenzene into styrene by removing hydrogen, while the Ethylbenzene Oxidation Method involves oxidizing ethylbenzene to produce styrene. Both methods result in the production of SM, which is widely used in the manufacturing of various products such as plastics, resins, rubber, and coatings. The choice of method depends on factors like cost, efficiency, and environmental considerations.</p></p>
