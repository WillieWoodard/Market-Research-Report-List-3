<p><p>The car ignition coil market comprises two main types: single-spark and multi-spark ignition systems. Single-spark systems generate one spark per ignition cycle, while multi-spark systems produce multiple sparks per cycle. Single-spark systems are common in older vehicles and provide a reliable ignition source. On the other hand, multi-spark systems are more advanced and offer improved engine performance, fuel efficiency, and emissions control. Both types cater to different consumer needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
