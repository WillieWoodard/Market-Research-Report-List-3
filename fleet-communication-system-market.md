<p><p>The Fleet Communication System market includes two main types: GNSS (Global Navigation Satellite System) and Cellular System. GNSS relies on satellite technology, enabling real-time tracking, navigation, and communication between fleets and control centers. It provides accurate positioning information, enhancing fleet management efficiency. On the other hand, Cellular Systems utilize mobile network infrastructure for fleet communication. They offer broader coverage areas, enabling fleets to remain connected even in remote locations. Both types play essential roles in optimizing fleet operations, ensuring better communication, and improving overall efficiency.</p></p>
