<p><p>Mid-infrared detector arrays are used in various applications such as thermal imaging, spectroscopy, and gas detection. There are three main types of detectors commonly used in the market - InSb, PbSe, and PbS. InSb detectors offer high sensitivity and fast response times, making them suitable for high-speed applications. PbSe detectors are known for their wide spectral response range and high performance in low light conditions. PbS detectors provide cost-effective solutions with good stability and low noise levels. Each type of detector has its own advantages and is selected based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
