<p><p>Virtual commissioning is a process used in manufacturing industries to validate and optimize the automation systems of a facility through the use of virtual models and simulations. It involves creating a digital replica of the manufacturing environment and using this simulation to test and fine-tune the automation systems before they are implemented in the physical facility. This allows for early identification and resolution of potential issues, significantly reducing the time and costs associated with commissioning and start-up.</p><p>The virtual commissioning market is expected to experience significant growth in the coming years. Factors contributing to this growth include the increasing adoption of automation technologies in various industries such as automotive, aerospace, and pharmaceuticals, as well as the need for reducing downtime and increasing productivity in manufacturing facilities.
