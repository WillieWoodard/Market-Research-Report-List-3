<p><p>The hospital intercom system market is divided into two types: wired system and wireless system. A wired system refers to an intercom system that utilizes physical connections, such as cables, to transmit audio signals between different areas of a hospital. On the other hand, a wireless system uses wireless technology, such as radio waves, to transmit these audio signals without the need for physical connections. Both systems serve the same purpose of facilitating communication within a hospital environment, but they differ in the way the audio signals are transmitted.</p></p>
