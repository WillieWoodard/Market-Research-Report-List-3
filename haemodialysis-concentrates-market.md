<p><p>Haemodialysis concentrates are used in the process of haemodialysis, a medical treatment for patients with kidney failure. Bicarbonate concentrates and Acid concentrates are two types of haemodialysis concentrates available in the market. Bicarbonate concentrates contain sodium bicarbonate and are used to neutralize excess acidity in the patient's blood during haemodialysis. Acid concentrates, on the other hand, contain acetic or citric acid and are used to remove excess alkalinity from the patient's blood. Both types of concentrates play a crucial role in maintaining the balance of electrolytes during haemodialysis.</p></p>
