<p><p>The automotive bicycle rack market offers various types of racks to transport bicycles. Hitch mounted racks are secured to the vehicle's hitch receiver and are known for their stability. Roof mounted racks are attached to the roof of the vehicle and provide easy access. Trunk mounted racks use straps to attach to the trunk or rear hatch and are more affordable. Pickup carriers are specifically designed for pickup trucks, allowing bicycles to be transported in the truck bed. Each type of rack offers different benefits and suits different vehicle types and user preferences.</p></p>
