<p><p>Automobile Lift Platform market includes various types such as Fixed Scissor Lift Platform and Rail Type Lifting Platform. Fixed Scissor Lift Platforms are stationary platforms that elevate vehicles using a scissor mechanism. They are commonly used in garages and workshops for maintenance and repair work. Rail Type Lifting Platforms, on the other hand, are mounted on tracks and can move horizontally as well as vertically, allowing for greater flexibility in positioning vehicles. Both types offer solutions for lifting vehicles safely and efficiently in different working environments.</p></p>
