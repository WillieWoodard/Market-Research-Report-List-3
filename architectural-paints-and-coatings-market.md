<p><p>Architectural paints and coatings market can be divided into three main types: water-borne, solvent-borne, and others. Water-borne paints and coatings are environmentally friendly, easy to clean and have low odor. Solvent-borne paints are durable, provide good coverage and adherence, but have higher levels of volatile organic compounds. The others category includes powder coatings, UV-cured coatings, and high-solids coatings. Each type has its own set of advantages and disadvantages, catering to different preferences and requirements in the architectural industry.</p></p>
