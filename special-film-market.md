<p><p>Special film market types cover a range of specific uses and properties. Stretch film is used for wrapping and securing products on pallets. Safety & security film enhances glass strength to prevent shattering. Anti-fog film prevents condensation on surfaces. Other films include UV-protective, biodegradable, and thermal films for various applications.</p></p>
