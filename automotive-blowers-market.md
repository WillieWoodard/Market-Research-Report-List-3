<p><ul><li>Single Wheel Blower</li><li>Double Wheel Blower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive blowers come in two primary types: single wheel blower and double wheel blower. A single wheel blower consists of one fan wheel for air circulation, making it more compact and efficient for smaller vehicles. On the other hand, a double wheel blower features two fan wheels for increased air volume and pressure, making it suitable for larger vehicles or applications requiring higher airflow. Both types are essential components in automotive heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems for regulating interior temperature and providing comfortable driving conditions.</p></p>
