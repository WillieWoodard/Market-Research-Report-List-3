<p><p>The angiography catheters market comprises various types of catheters used in medical procedures for diagnosing and treating heart conditions. Scoring balloon catheters are designed to help in the treatment of blocked arteries. Conventional catheters are the standard type used for angiography procedures. DEB (drug-eluting balloon) catheters are coated with medication to prevent restenosis. Cutting balloon catheters have small blades to help in opening blocked arteries. These different types of catheters play a crucial role in performing angiography procedures and improving patient outcomes.</p></p>
