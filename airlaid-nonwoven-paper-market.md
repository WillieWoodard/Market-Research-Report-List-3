<p><p>Airlaid Nonwoven Paper can be classified into three main types - Latex-bonded Airlaid Paper, Thermal-bonded Airlaid Paper, and Multi-bonded Airlaid Paper. Latex-bonded Airlaid Paper is created by bonding fibers using latex, resulting in a soft and absorbent material. Thermal-bonded Airlaid Paper uses heat to bond fibers together, making it strong and durable. Multi-bonded Airlaid Paper incorporates a combination of bonding methods to achieve specific characteristics such as absorbency, strength, and softness. Each type has its own unique advantages and applications in different industries.</p></p>
