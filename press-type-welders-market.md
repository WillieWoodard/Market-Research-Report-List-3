<p><p>Press type welders are used in various industries for different types of welding processes. Projection welding is a type of resistance welding where the weld is localized by means of raised sections, or projections, on one or both of the parts to be joined. Spot welding is another common type where two metal surfaces are joined together by applying pressure and passing electrical current through the materials. Both types of welding are commonly used in automotive, aerospace, and manufacturing industries for joining metal parts quickly and efficiently.</p></p>
