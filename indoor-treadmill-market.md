<p><p>The indoor treadmill market can be divided into two main types: motorised treadmills and no-motorised treadmills. Motorised treadmills are powered by an electric motor, allowing users to set their desired speed and incline level. They usually come with various workout programs and features for a more interactive experience. On the other hand, no-motorised treadmills rely solely on the user's effort to move the belt. These treadmills are often manually operated and do not offer adjustable speeds or inclines. Both types cater to different preferences and fitness goals.</p></p>
