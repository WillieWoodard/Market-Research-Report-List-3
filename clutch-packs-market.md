<p><ul><li>Single Disc</li><li>Multi Disc</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single disc clutch packs are typically used in passenger vehicles and light duty trucks, offering a simple and compact design with a single friction disc. On the other hand, multi-disc clutch packs are commonly found in high performance vehicles and heavy duty applications, providing increased torque handling capacity and improved heat dissipation due to the presence of multiple friction discs. Both types of clutch packs play a crucial role in transmitting power from the engine to the transmission, allowing for smooth engagement and disengagement of the vehicle's drivetrain.</p></p>
