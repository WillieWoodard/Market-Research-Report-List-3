<p><p>Veterinary hematology analyzers market encompasses fully automatic and semi-automatic analyzers. These analyzers offer high throughput, accuracy, and efficiency, making them suitable for large veterinary clinics or laboratories with high sample volumes. On the other hand, semi-automatic analyzers require some manual intervention, such as sample preparation, and usually offer lower throughput. These analyzers may be more suitable for smaller veterinary practices with lower sample volumes.</p></p>
