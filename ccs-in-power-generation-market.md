Oxy-fuel combustion capture utilizes pure oxygen instead of air to burn fuel, resulting in a flue gas consisting mainly of carbon dioxide. These methods aim to reduce greenhouse gas emissions and mitigate climate change by preventing carbon dioxide from being released into the atmosphere.</p></p>
