<p><strong>The Harmonic Damper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Elastomer Damper</li><li>Viscous Damper</li><li>Friction-style Damper</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Harmonic dampers are used to reduce vibration and torsional crankshaft twisting in internal combustion engines. There are three main types in the market: elastomer dampers, viscous dampers, and friction-style dampers. Elastomer dampers use rubber or synthetic material to absorb and dampen vibrations. Viscous dampers use fluid to dissipate vibrations. Friction-style dampers use friction to control torsional vibrations. Each type has its advantages and is used in different applications based on the specific needs of the engine or machinery.</p></p>
