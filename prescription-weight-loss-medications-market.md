<p><p>Prescription weight loss medications are drugs that are prescribed by healthcare professionals to assist individuals in losing weight. Factors contributing to this growth include the increasing prevalence of obesity worldwide, growing adoption of sedentary lifestyles, and rising awareness about the health risks associated with obesity.</p><p>Moreover, the market is witnessing several trends that are influencing its growth. For instance, there is a shift towards the development of combination therapies, where two or more medications are used together to enhance weight loss outcomes. This trend is driven by the belief that combining different mechanisms of action can lead to better results.</p><p>Additionally, there is a growing interest in the use of novel formulations such as extended-release tablets and injectables.
