<p><p>Room pressure controllers come in two main types: fixed and portable. Fixed room pressure controllers are permanently installed within a room and are designed to regulate and maintain the air pressure within that specific space. On the other hand, portable room pressure controllers are designed to be easily moved from room to room as needed, offering flexibility in managing air pressure levels in various spaces. Both types of controllers play a crucial role in ensuring optimal air quality and safety within buildings.</p></p>
