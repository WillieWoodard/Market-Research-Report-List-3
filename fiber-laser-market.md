<p><strong>The Fiber Laser Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser</li><li>Pulsed Fiber Laser</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fiber Laser market consists of two main types: Continuous Wave (CW) Fiber Laser and Pulsed Fiber Laser. Continuous Wave Fiber Lasers emit a continuous laser beam, making them suitable for applications requiring a constant energy output, such as material processing and laser cutting. On the other hand, Pulsed Fiber Lasers emit laser pulses at high peak powers for short durations, making them ideal for applications like marking, engraving, and precision machining where high precision and peak power are necessary. Both types cater to different industrial needs within the Fiber Laser market.</p></p>
