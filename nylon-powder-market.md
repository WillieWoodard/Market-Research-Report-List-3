<p><p>Nylon powder finds application in various industries such as selective laser sintering (3D printing), electrostatic spraying, fluid bed coating, high-end coatings, and the cosmetics market. In selective laser sintering, the powder is melted and solidified layer by layer to create complex 3D printed objects. Electrostatic spraying involves using an electric charge to apply a uniform coating of nylon powder onto a surface. Fluid bed coating uses a fluidized bed to apply a coating of nylon powder onto substrates. High-end coatings utilize nylon powder to create durable and corrosion-resistant finishes. In the cosmetics market, nylon powder is used in products such as makeup and skincare to provide a smooth and matte finish.</p></p>
