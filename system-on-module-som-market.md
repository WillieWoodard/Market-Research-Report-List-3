<p><p>A System on Module (SOM) is a small form factor computer module that integrates a processor, memory, storage, and other essential components into a single compact package. SOMs are used to speed up the development process of embedded systems by providing a standardized platform that can be easily integrated into a custom-designed carrier board.</p><p>The System on Module (SOM) market is experiencing significant growth due to the increasing demand for compact, power-efficient, and cost-effective embedded solutions across various industries such as healthcare, industrial automation, automotive, and consumer electronics. The adoption of SOMs is also being driven by the need for faster time-to-market and reduced development costs for IoT and connected devices.</p><p>Key trends in the System on Module (SOM) market include the shift towards more advanced and powerful processors, the increasing use of SOMs in edge computing applications, and the integration of artificial intelligence capabilities into SOMs. Additionally, the rising adoption of wireless connectivity options such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth in SOMs is also driving market growth.</p><p>Overall, the System on Module (SOM) Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 15% during the forecast period, fueled by the increasing demand for connected devices and the need for efficient embedded solutions.</p></p>
