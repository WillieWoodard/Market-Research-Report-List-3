<p><p>The medicinal mushroom extracts market includes two types: powder and liquid. Powder extracts are obtained by drying and grinding mushrooms into a fine powder, which can be easily added to food, beverages, or supplements. Liquid extracts are created by steeping or boiling mushrooms in water or alcohol to obtain a concentrated liquid form that can be consumed directly or added to various products. Both types offer a convenient way to absorb the beneficial compounds present in medicinal mushrooms and are popular choices for individuals seeking natural health remedies.</p></p>
