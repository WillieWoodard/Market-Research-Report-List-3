This growth can be attributed to several factors. Firstly, the increasing prevalence of respiratory disorders in children is driving the demand for effective and convenient treatment options. Additionally, the use of lightweight materials and compact designs is gaining traction, making the devices more portable and convenient for children and their caregivers.</p><p>In conclusion, the electronic pediatric nebulizer market is witnessing significant growth due to the increasing prevalence of respiratory disorders in children, advancements in technology, and rising awareness about early diagnosis and treatment.
