<p><ul><li>Single-sided</li><li>Double-sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ultra thin FPC (Flexible Printed Circuit) market is divided into single-sided and double-sided types. Single-sided FPCs have conductive traces on only one side of the flexible substrate, making them ideal for simpler electronic applications. On the other hand, double-sided FPCs have conductive traces on both sides of the substrate, allowing for more complex circuit designs and higher density of components. Both types offer flexibility, space savings, and lightweight characteristics, making them suitable for various electronic devices and applications.</p></p>
