There are two main types of hot-pressed magnets: hot-pressed isotropic neodymium iron boron magnets and hot extrusion anisotropic neodymium iron boron magnets. Isotropic magnets have equal magnetic properties in all directions, while anisotropic magnets have preferred magnetic orientations. Both types are known for their high magnetic strength and are commonly used in various industrial applications due to their superior magnetic properties and performance.</p></p>
