<p><p>Stainless steel fiber market can be segmented into three main types based on their physical attributes and application suitability. Flat stainless steel fiber is characterized by its smooth surface, while Hooked stainless steel fiber has small hooks or bends along its length for increased bonding capacity. Undulated stainless steel fiber features a wavy pattern which enhances its ability to interlock with surrounding materials. Each type of stainless steel fiber serves specific purposes in industries like construction, automotive, and aerospace, depending on the requirements of the project.</p></p>
