<p><p>The display device market is comprised of various types, including LCD (liquid crystal display), LED (light-emitting diode), OLED (organic light-emitting diode), AMOLED (active-matrix organic light-emitting diode), and others. LCD displays use a backlight to illuminate pixels and offer good image quality but require a constant light source. LED displays are similar to LCDs but use LEDs as a backlight, providing energy efficiency and enhanced brightness. OLED displays utilize organic compounds that emit light when an electric current passes through them, resulting in better color accuracy and higher contrast. AMOLED is an advanced version of OLED, offering faster response times and improved energy efficiency. Other display types include e-paper and quantum dot displays.</p></p>
