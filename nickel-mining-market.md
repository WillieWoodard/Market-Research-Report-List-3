<p><ul><li>Pentlandite (Sulfide Ore)</li><li>Laterite-Nickel Ore</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Nickel mining involves extracting nickel from mineral deposits, with two main market types being Pentlandite (Sulfide Ore) and Laterite-Nickel Ore. Pentlandite is a sulfide ore that contains nickel and other valuable minerals. It is usually mined underground and requires more complex processing methods. Laterite-Nickel Ore refers to a group of nickel-rich oxide ores found near the surface, often in tropical regions. It is typically open-pit mined and requires less processing. Both market types contribute to the global supply of nickel, which is used in various industries like stainless steel production and electric vehicle batteries.</p></p>
