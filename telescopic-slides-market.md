<p><p>Telescopic slides are available in two main types: Full Extension Type and Partial Extension Type. Full Extension Type slides allow the drawer or platform to fully extend out of the cabinet or enclosure, providing easy access to all its contents. Partial Extension Type slides, on the other hand, only allow the drawer or platform to extend partially, leaving some contents hidden within the cabinet. The choice between these two types depends on the specific application and space requirements of the user.</p></p>
