<p><p>The Endoscopic Stricture Management Device market includes different types of devices for treating strictures in the body. Balloon dilators are inflatable devices that expand to widen the narrowing area. Stents are small expandable tubes inserted into the stricture to provide support and maintain the opening. Bougie dilators are solid metal rods used to stretch and dilate the stricture. These devices are used in endoscopic procedures to alleviate strictures and improve the flow of fluids or other substances in the body.</p></p>
