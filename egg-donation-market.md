<p><strong>The Egg Donation Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fresh Egg</li><li>Frozen Egg</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The egg donation market consists of two main types: fresh egg and frozen egg. Fresh egg donation involves the retrieval of eggs from a donor and their immediate fertilization with the recipient's sperm. This process usually occurs in real-time and requires synchronization of the menstrual cycles of the donor and recipient. On the other hand, frozen egg donation involves the retrieval and cryopreservation of eggs from a donor, which are later thawed and fertilized with the recipient's sperm at a time of their choosing. Frozen egg donation offers more flexibility in terms of timing and allows for easier coordination between donors and recipients.</p></p>
