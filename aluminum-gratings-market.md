<p><strong>The Aluminum Gratings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Press-locked Gratings</li><li>Swage-locked Gratings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum gratings come in different types, with press-locked gratings and swage-locked gratings being popular choices. Press-locked gratings are manufactured by permanently locking crossbars into bearing bars through intense hydraulic pressure, providing a sturdy and secure structure. On the other hand, swage-locked gratings are assembled by interlocking the bars through a mechanical process, offering excellent lateral stability and high corrosion resistance. Both types of gratings are widely used in industrial, commercial, and residential applications due to their strength, durability, and versatility.</p></p>
