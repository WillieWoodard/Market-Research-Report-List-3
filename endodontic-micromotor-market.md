<p><p>Endodontic micromotors are dental devices used in root canal treatments. Wired micromotors require a physical connection to a power source for operation, providing consistent power but limiting mobility. On the other hand, wireless micromotors operate without a physical connection, relying on battery power and enhancing portability. Both types offer precise and controlled movement for efficient root canal procedures. The choice between wired and wireless micromotors depends on the dentist's preference and the specific needs of the treatment.</p></p>
