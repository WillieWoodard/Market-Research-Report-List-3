These technologies enhance the precision and clarity of images, enabling surgeons to perform complex procedures with increased accuracy.</p><p>Another trend is the introduction of wireless HD surgical monitors, which eliminate the need for cables and wires in the operating room. These monitors provide flexibility and ease of use, improving the overall efficiency of surgical procedures.</p><p>Additionally, market players are focusing on developing lightweight and portable HD surgical monitors that can be easily moved and installed in different operating rooms.
