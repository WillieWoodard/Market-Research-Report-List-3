<p><p>Flexible graphite seal material market includes two main types: Plain Graphite Type and Stainless Steel Flexible Graphite Type. Plain Graphite Type is a cost-effective option made purely from graphite, providing high flexibility and thermal conductivity. On the other hand, Stainless Steel Flexible Graphite Type incorporates stainless steel layers for enhanced durability and strength. Both types offer excellent sealing properties for various industrial applications, but the Stainless Steel Flexible Graphite Type is preferred in high-pressure and high-temperature environments due to its added reinforcement.</p></p>
