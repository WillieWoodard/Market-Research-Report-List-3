Engine Analyzer Type machines are used for diagnosing engine-related issues by analyzing various parameters such as emissions, fuel efficiency, and performance. Analog Camera Type machines use cameras to visually inspect different parts of the vehicle for any defects or irregularities. Code Reader Type machines read and interpret error codes from the vehicle's onboard computer to identify and troubleshoot malfunctions. Each type plays a crucial role in ensuring the safety and performance of vehicles.</p></p>
