<p><p>Polycarbonate Track Etched (PCTE) membranes are available in two main types: White and Black. The White PCTE membrane is used for applications where visibility or contrast is important, such as microscopy or imaging. On the other hand, the Black PCTE membrane is commonly used for applications where light blocking or absorption is necessary, such as in fluorescence studies or particle counting. Both types offer high porosity, precise pore sizes, and excellent chemical resistance for various filtration and separation applications.</p></p>
