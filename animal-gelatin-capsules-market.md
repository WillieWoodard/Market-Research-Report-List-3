<p><p>Animal gelatin capsules are classified into three main types based on the source of gelatin used: bovine, porcine, and piscine. Bovine gelatin capsules are made from cow hides and bones, porcine gelatin capsules are derived from pig skin and bones, while piscine gelatin capsules are sourced from fish scales and skin. Each type of gelatin has its own unique properties and applications, catering to different consumer preferences and dietary restrictions. The market for these animal gelatin capsules continues to grow as more people seek out natural and pharmaceutical-grade options for their supplement needs.</p></p>
