<p><p>High strength bolts can be categorized into different types based on their design and usage. Hexagon bolts are commonly used in construction and manufacturing industries due to their hexagonal head which provides a secure grip. Buttonhead bolts have a rounded head for a flush finish and are often used in furniture assembly. Coach bolts are specifically designed for joining heavy timber pieces together. Plow bolts are used in agricultural and construction machinery for their durability. Other types of high strength bolts include shoulder bolts, socket head bolts, and U-bolts, each serving specific purposes in various industries.</p></p>
